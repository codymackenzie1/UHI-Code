Rules of Pontoon -
The player recieves two random cards at the start of the game.
The house draws two cards, one of which is face up and the other is face down.
This is to help the player somewhat decide,
if they want to draw to try and beat the house or not.
The players objective is too get 21, however the user can beat the house and still win.
The user beats the house if they draw higher and don't go bust.
Pontoon's rules are very similar too 'Blackjack' & '21'.
